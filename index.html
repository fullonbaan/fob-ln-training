FAQs
Frequently Asked Questions
Everything you need to know before enrolling in our Infor LN training programs.

How is the training delivered?

+
All training is conducted live and online via screen-sharing sessions (Zoom / Google Meet / Teams). It's 1-on-1 with a dedicated trainer — not pre-recorded videos. You interact in real time, ask questions, and work hands-on in a live Infor LN environment.

Can I choose a specific module or do I have to take all?

+
Absolutely — you can enroll for individual modules (e.g., only Finance or only ION) or combine multiple modules into a custom learning path. There's no requirement to take the full program. We'll help you pick based on your career goals.

What are the prerequisites for enrolling?

+
No prior Infor LN experience is required for functional modules — they start from basics. For technical modules (Tools & Development, Extensibility), a general understanding of ERP concepts and basic programming logic is helpful but not mandatory. Our trainer adjusts the pace to your level.

What is the schedule like? Can I take weekend classes?

+
Scheduling is fully flexible. You can opt for weekday sessions, weekend-only batches, or a combination — whatever suits your availability. Sessions are typically 1–2 hours per sitting. We coordinate across time zones for international students.

Do I get a certificate after completing the training?

+
Yes. Upon successful completion of each module, you receive a FullOnBaan Certificate of Completion — a verifiable digital credential you can add to your LinkedIn profile and resume. Certificates are issued per module.

Is job assistance really included?

+
Yes — every enrollment includes job assistance. This covers resume building, mock interviews, and access to our WhatsApp community where Infor LN job opportunities are shared regularly. Many of our alumni have been placed within 1–3 months of completing their training.

Do you provide access to an Infor LN environment for practice?

+
During training sessions, the trainer demonstrates and walks you through exercises on a live Infor LN environment. For self-practice, we provide guidance on setting up sandbox environments. The training is designed to be hands-on, not just theoretical.

What if I need to pause or reschedule my training?

+
We understand that schedules can change. You can pause your training and resume later without losing progress. Rescheduling individual sessions is also easy — just inform your trainer 24 hours in advance. We're here to make it work for you.

How do I enroll or inquire about pricing?

+
Simply fill out the inquiry form on this page or reach us on WhatsApp at +91 63605 93122 or email fullonbaan@gmail.com. We'll share the training plan, pricing details, and available slots within 24 hours. No commitments required to inquire.

Who is the trainer and what is their experience?

+
Our lead trainer has 10+ years of hands-on experience implementing and supporting Infor LN / Baan ERP across multiple industries. The curriculum is built from real project scenarios — not textbook theory. You learn what's actually used on the job.
